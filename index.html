    Overview

    Problem solving is an essential skill set for the future. The course is designed to help facilitate this opportunity for learners, young and adult. It is restricted to mathematical problems, even though problem solving and critical thinking have a wider goal. The problems are set in situations that learners are familiar with, which will help them see the relevance of mathematics in real life situations. They are created to stimulate discussions and can be used by learners as springboards for investigation through visual thinking habits. It is predicated on the idea that studying mathematics can generate the same enthusiasm as a team sport, without necessarily being competitive. The aim is to empower the learners to become master problem solvers that will help them become creative thinkers and eventually change makers for the future. We introduce learners to the art of thinking or metacognition through a series of personalized mathematical problems and their underlying theories.

    How will it work?

    This is a fully online course where each student is guided through a weekly assignment and provided with tasks to complete, which are then assessed by the instructor and returned with feedback. The weekly assignments will be tailored to individual learning needs so that each student, regardless of her/his age or ability can be engaged and challenged with her/his learning. Students will be provided with two to three problems, tailored according to the standard, the learning speed of the student etc. If a student is unable to make progress, she/he will be given a hint. If even after that the student is unable to make progress, a suggested solution with detailed explanation is provided. The underlying theory will be also be introduced through a series of these problems.
